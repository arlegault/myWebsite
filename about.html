I'm Alex

I like building and learning. I get to do both of those things through a career in product management. For the past decade+, I've been leading product teams at PitchBook Data and Carta- Fund Admin building for private markets investors. My "sweet spot" has been joining teams in the 10-15M ARR range and scaling them to 9 figures. Currently, I serve as a Senior Director of Product at Carta.

Prior, I worked at early stage stage startups in Seattle and more tranditional companies like State Farm in a marketing capacity.

My degree is in psychology from the University of Washington where I focused on what infleucnes which neural pathways are used in moral decision-making as well as sports psychology. For fun, I also spent a year living in Bogota, Colombia meeting family and improving my spanish.

Beyond the office, I build and learn through hobbies like reading, fishing, hunting, skiing, and flying. I use this website as a sort of diary that has the added benefit of letting other people get to know me.
